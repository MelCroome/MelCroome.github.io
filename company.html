Recognised leader in the building and construction in Tasmania

A Tasmanian family-owned and operated business in the State's North West, AJ & M Construction has steadily grown to become a recognised leader in the building and construction industry. Our success in the Tasmanian market has enabled us to expand the business further and undertake selected projects statewide and interstate.

AJ & M Construction operates throughout Tasmania on Civil, Industrial, Commercial and Residential Projects.


Quality & Safety

AJ & M Construction Pty Ltd maintains an Integrated Management System (IMS), and is accredited for construction and project management in the civil, commercial/industrial and residential industries in accordance with Quality Management Systems, Environmental Management Systems and Occupational health and Safety Requirements
